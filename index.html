Urban Heat Island Facts

  • Cities are usually 1–7°C hotter than nearby rural areas.
  • Concrete and asphalt absorb and store heat.
  • Lack of vegetation increases urban temperature.
  • Urban trees and green roofs help reduce heat.
  • Urban Heat Island effect impacts climate and human health.
